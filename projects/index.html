Projects

A selection of research and engineering projects I've worked on.

GamiBench

Benchmark for 22 MLLMs/VLMs on multi-modal 2D-to-3D spatial reasoning (3x AAAI, Springer-Nature 2026).

InvasiveWatch

Species invasiveness recommender: React/Vite, FastAPI, FAISS, iNaturalist, Mapbox GL (1st Place DS3).

SmartRation

CV web app for meal planning from receipts: TypeScript, Supabase, Google Vision, Claude (Berkeley AI Hackathon).

Weak-to-Strong ICL

Weak/strong GPT pairs on MATH with async OpenAI evaluation and weak-bank caching in Colab.

IEEE Embedded AI

SLMs for speech-to-text on ESP32-class devices.

LoL Win Factors

EDA and ensemble models on a 200k-row League of Legends dataset (pandas, scikit-learn).
